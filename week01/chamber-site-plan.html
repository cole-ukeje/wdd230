Lagos Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Site Name

Lagos Chamber of Commerce: This name represents the state's business community and economic hub, aiming to support local businesses and international investments.

Site Purpose

  • To promote business networking, economic growth, and investment opportunities in Lagos, Nigeria.
  • To provide resources, events, and support for local businesses and entrepreneurs.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming networking events are available for local businesses?
  • How can I register my business with the chamber?
  • What are the latest economic trends in Lagos?

Color Schema

  • Dark Blue (#0A3D62): Used for headers and navigation.
  • Earthy Green (#A3CB38): Used for buttons, accents, and call-to-action sections.
  • Light Beige (#F5F5DC): Background for readability and aesthetic balance.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings (bold and modern).
  • Open Sans: Used for body text (clean and readable).
